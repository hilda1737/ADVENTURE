It was quite the year for death-defying adventures and record-breaking feats by athletes around the world. From skiers riding away from avalanches to surfers taking on massive waves and bikers riding down seemingly impossible paths, there were plenty of awe-inspiring videos out there to get your heart racing. We scoured the web to find the best adventure videos of the year, and these will make you want to get out into the world and find some adventure yourself.

Surfer Tom Butler Rides Gigantic Wave, Possibly Sets New World Record

There might be a new world record for 2018—although it likely won’t be official until 2019. After Rodrigo Koxa set a record by riding an 80-foot wave, Butler may have taken on an even bigger one in Nazaré, the spot where Koxa set his record. This wave was reportedly around 100-feet tall, and while that’s not official yet, watch and see for yourself. Once this wave ride becomes official, we’ll make sure to update.

Football Freestyler John Farnworth Juggles Above the Austrian Alps

In what was ranked as GoPro’s top video of 2018, multiple Guinness World Record holder and football freestyler John Farnworth hangs off of a hot air balloon thousands of feet above the Austrian Alps and has some fun with a soccer ball. From juggling with his feet and balancing on his head, Farnworth pulls off some incredible tricks.

Surfing Legend Kelly Slater Pulls Off Unbelievable Recovery

Surfer Kelly Slater doing something incredible on a wave usually isn’t news considering all he’s done through his lengthy career, but this video is worth spotlighting. Here, Slater gets knocked off his board while riding a wave at the Pipe Masters competition in Hawaii, and somehow he’s able to recover and finish off the ride.

Wingsuit Flyers BASE Jump and Land In Flying Plane

The title of this one says it all: “Soul Flyers” Fred Fugen and Vince Reffet BASE jumped off the top of the Jungfrau mountain in Switzerland—and then landed in a flying plane that was circling below.
